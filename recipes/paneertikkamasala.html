Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer Tikka Masala is a dish of Indian cuisine. It is a combination of a dumpling, a filling, and a gravy. It is a dish that is popular in the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients needed for Paneer Tikka Masala

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Coriander
  • Pepper
  • Curry Powder
  • Butter

Steps to make Paneer Tikka Masala

  1. In a large bowl, combine the paneer, yogurt, salt, garlic, onion, tomato, coriander, pepper, curry powder and butter.
  2. Mix well.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the butter and oil.
  4. When the butter and oil are hot, add the paneer mixture and cook on medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from the heat and add the coriander leaves.
  6. Your Paneer Tikka Masala is ready to serve.
